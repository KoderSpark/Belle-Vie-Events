# haus of veloura Brand Guidelines

## Brand Overview
haus of veloura is a luxury event design and decor company that embraces **minimalist aesthetics** combined with elegant sophistication. Our visual identity reflects simplicity, clarity, and timeless elegance.

---

## Color Palette

### Brand Colors (2 Color Palette Only)

#### Warm Taupe Brown
- **Hex:** `#89725B`
- **RGB:** 137, 114, 91
- **Use:** Primary accent color, typography, key UI elements, decorative elements

#### Black
- **Hex:** `#000000`
- **RGB:** 0, 0, 0
- **Use:** Primary text, strong contrast, structural elements, borders

---

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Simple Two-Color Approach (Dark Theme)

**Color Distribution:**
- **Black (#000000)** - Primary dark background (60-70% of design)
- **Warm Taupe Brown (#89725B)** - Accent highlights and key elements (15-20% of design)
- **White (#FFFFFF)** - Text and light accents (15-25% of design)

### Usage by Medium

#### Digital Graphics & Social Media Posts
```
Background:           #000000 (black) or very dark
Accent Elements:      #89725B (strategic placement)
Text/Structure:       #FFFFFF (white for readability)
Decorative Lines:     #89725B (1-3px borders/accents)
```

#### Advertisement Materials
```
Background:           #000000 (black) or dark
Accent Colors:        #89725B (highlights, lines, shapes)
Light Elements:       #FFFFFF (white text, borders, structure)
Approach:             Clean, minimal, use ample dark space
```

---

## Design Principles

### Minimalist Aesthetic Core Values

1. **Simplicity**
   - Clean lines and uncluttered layouts
   - Maximum dark space (negative space is essential)
   - Remove all non-essential elements

2. **Clarity**
   - Clear typography hierarchy using white text
   - High contrast for readability on dark backgrounds
   - Intentional visual hierarchy

3. **Elegance**
   - Premium feel through restraint, not excess
   - Strategic use of warm taupe accent color
   - Quality white typography

4. **Consistency**
   - Uniform spacing and alignment
   - Consistent use of the dark theme palette only
   - Predictable visual patterns

## Typography

### Font Pairing - Dark Theme

**Headers:** Playfair Display (Serif)
- Elegant, luxury feel
- Use for brand name and main headlines
- White (#FFFFFF) - primary choice

**Body Text:** Inter (Sans-serif)
- Clean, modern, highly readable
- White (#FFFFFF) for all text
- Use for all typography

## Social Media Content Guidelines

### Instagram Posts

**Color Scheme:**
- Dominant: Dark/Black (#000000) background
- Accent: Warm Taupe Brown (#89725B) strategically placed
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) for readability

**Template Structure:**
1. Dark/Black background
2. 15-20% Warm taupe accent elements or border
3. White text with generous padding

**Example Layouts:**
- Full dark background with centered taupe accent line
- Dark background with taupe corner element
- Image with dark border + white text overlay

### Facebook Covers & Event Promotions

**Dimensions:** 1200 x 628 px

**Layout Formula:**
- 60% Image or dark background
- 30-40% Black text/copy
- 15-20% Warm Taupe accents (lines, shapes, badges)

### Stories & Reels

**Color Overlay:**
- Use dark background or overlay
- Add warm taupe accent elements
- Use white text for maximum readability

### Caption & Copy Guidelines

- Use short, impactful sentences
- Lead with the most important information
- Pair with minimalist graphic - avoid text clutter
- Only use white text and taupe accents (no other colors)

## Advertisement Materials

### Print Advertisements

**Standard Ad Sizes:**
- Full Page: 8.5" x 11"
- Half Page: 8.5" x 5.5"
- Quarter Page: 4.25" x 5.5"

**Layout Guidelines:**
1. 60-70% dark/black background
2. 20% Image/visual content (high quality, clean)
3. 15-25% White text/information
4. 10-20% Warm Taupe accents

**Material Examples:**
- Magazine ads: Clean dark layout with generous dark space
- Direct mail: Dark background with taupe accents
- Digital banners: Minimal text, strong focal point in taupe

### Digital Display Ads

**Standard Sizes:**
- 300 x 250 px (Medium Rectangle)
- 728 x 90 px (Leaderboard)
- 970 x 250 px (Billboard)

**Formula:**
- 70-80% Dark background
- 15-20% White text/CTA
- 10-20% Warm Taupe accents (button, underline, border)

## Visual Elements

### Lines & Borders
- Use warm taupe (#89725B) or white for decorative lines
- Thickness: 1-3px
- Full-width or intentional short accents

### Shapes & Icons
- Geometric shapes: Minimal, clean lines
- Color: Warm taupe (#89725B) or white only
- Background: Transparent or dark only

### Buttons & CTAs
- Background: Warm Taupe Brown (#89725B)
- Text: White (#FFFFFF) or #000000 for contrast
- Hover State: Lighter taupe or white background
- Padding: Generous (minimum 12px vertical)

### Images
- Black & white or desaturated photography preferred
- Warm lighting (golden hour preference)
- High contrast, sharp focus
- Minimal styling overlays

---

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Avoid:**
- Cluttered layouts with too many elements
- Using colors outside the palette (#89725B, #000000, and #FFFFFF)
- Poor contrast between text and background
- Excessive text in graphics
- Adding additional accent colors beyond the palette
- Ornate or overly decorative elements
- Inconsistent spacing

✅ **Do:**
- Prioritize dark space and breathing room
- Use ONLY #89725B (taupe), #000000 (dark), and #FFFFFF (white)
- Maintain clean, professional dark theme appearance
- Test contrast ratios for readability on dark backgrounds
- Ensure white text has sufficient contrast against dark backgrounds
- Keep it simple and elegant
- Stick to the three-element palette strictly (dark, taupe, white)
