## Visual Hierarchy for Posts

### Importance Levels

**1. HERO IMAGE** (60-70% of post)
- High quality, well-lit, minimal clutter
- Clear focal point
- Professional photography

**2. PRIMARY TEXT** (20-25%)
- Main message or event title
- Large, bold (Playfair Display)
- White color for maximum readability

**3. ACCENTS** (10-15%)
- Taupe decorative elements
- Lines, subtle shapes
- Support the primary message

**4. SECONDARY TEXT** (Minimal)
- Details, dates, locations
- Smaller type (Inter)
- White (#FFFFFF) text only

## Caption Writing Guidelines

### For Maximum Engagement

✅ **DO:**
- Start with emoji (relevant to content)
- Lead with the most compelling information
- Include location and date when relevant
- Ask a question or encourage comment
- End with 2-3 hashtags minimum
- Keep under 150 characters for first line (if possible)
- Use line breaks for readability

❌ **DON'T:**
- Use excessive hashtags (15+ looks spammy)
- Write in ALL CAPS
- Use multiple exclamation marks
- Share repetitive content within same week
- Post without proofreading for brand voice

### Caption Formula

**Line 1:** Hook + Emoji (compelling opener)
**Line 2-3:** Value or story (why this matters)
**Line 4:** Call-to-action (engagement or inquiry)
**Line 5:** 2-5 Hashtags (strategic and branded)

## Color Usage in Social Posts

### Instagram Feed Aesthetic - Dark Theme

**Grid Goal:** Cohesive, elegant, minimalist dark appearance

**Color Deployment:**
- 5-7 posts: Predominantly dark background
- 2-3 posts: Taupe accent focus
- 1-2 posts: White accent or dramatic imagery

**Ratio Per Post:**
- 70-80% Image or dark space
- 15-20% White text/elements
- 5-10% Taupe accents

### Visual Consistency Tips

1. **Use consistent borders** (dark or subtle taupe)
2. **Maintain centered composition** (symmetrical layouts read cleaner)
3. **Keep text overlay consistent** (same fonts, sizes, positions, white text)
4. **Repeat accent elements** (same line thickness, corner placements with taupe)
5. **Color block previews** (3-4 posts with similar dark + taupe placement)
